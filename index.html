The Problem

Running an integration shop on tools built for everyone else.

Generic CRMs don't know what an EOAT is. Project software can't estimate a cycle time. Quoting tools can't link a quote to the parts it consumes. So you stitch it together with spreadsheets, Outlook threads, and tribal knowledge — and lose visibility on the only number that matters: are we making money on this cell?

Quotes that take days, not hours

Every cell is custom — robot, EOAT, vision, safety, controls, integration hours. By the time you've built the quote, the customer's gone cold or the scope has shifted.

No idea what each cell costs

Labour, parts, travel, programming hours all live in different places. By the time the project closes, you've forgotten what was quoted versus what was spent — and the next quote repeats the same mistake.

Cycle-time estimates are half guess, half whiteboard

Customers want cycle times in the proposal. Estimating them properly means modelling the cell. Most shops eyeball it, pad it, and hope — sometimes that's the difference between winning the job and losing money on it.

Each STAI Hub is configured for how an integrator actually operates — from the first cell quote to the install report. You get the modules you need (cycle-time estimation, cell quoting, parts tracking, install SOPs, scheduling, profit tracking), branded for your shop, integrated from day one.

Modules

Pick the modules that match how you build cells.

Every HUB starts with the integrator core and grows from there. Each module is a full tool, built to work standalone and to share data with every other module in your HUB — so a quoted part becomes a tracked part becomes an installed part.

Integrator flagship

TACTIME

Upload a DXF, get a realistic robotic cycle-time estimate. Turns guess-and-pad into numbers you can put on a quote — and PDF reports you can hand to the customer.

JOBS

Cell-by-cell project tracking with quotes, POs, expenses, and per-cell profit. The number every shop owner actually cares about, finally visible.

STOCK

Parts and asset tracking for the components you build cells from — robots, sensors, EOAT, pneumatics — with reorder alerts and AI-drafted supplier replenishment.

SOP

Site work instructions, commissioning checklists, install SOPs — generated from a phone video, branded for your shop, ready for the customer.

CAL

Schedule techs and projects across builds, installs, and commissioning — colour-coded by cell, visible to the whole shop.

TIMESHEET

Hours against the cell, not the calendar. Labour cost rolls straight into JOBS so per-cell profit numbers are always current.

VERSE

A spatial command center for everything in flight — proposals, active builds, commissioning, support — laid out so the whole shop sees what matters this week.

Additional modules available, and custom modules can be built for the specific way your shop works — usually in days, not months.
