Is This For You?

Honest positioning. We're not for everyone.

Great fit if…

  • •You run a small to medium-sized business (1–50 people).
  • •You're running operations on spreadsheets, email, and a patchwork of SaaS tools.
  • •You want software shaped around your business, not the other way around.

Not a fit if…
