So we built the same thing, better — custom to their workflow, tied into their existing systems, easier for their team to use. Beautiful documents in minutes. Same output, fraction of the cost, and no more annual renewal fees.

One person. Less than a week. Now they own the tool outright.

STOCK

Live

Parts were tracked in an Excel spreadsheet. A piece of paper taped to the stockroom door was the "system" — log items in and out with a pen. No matter how many reminders went out, nobody picked up the pen. Items went missing. Stock ran out without warning. Projects got held up waiting on materials that should have been on the shelf.

Instead of locking into expensive inventory software, we built a simple stock tracking module right into their HUB. Imported the old spreadsheet directly. Integrated a cheap barcode scanner. Now scan in, scan out — a pop-up confirms the transaction. Everyone uses it because it's faster than not using it.

Then we added an AI layer on top. When an item drops below its reorder threshold, the system drafts a replenishment email to the supplier requesting a quote. When the quote comes back, the AI parses it, logs the pricing, and flags it for approval. One click to confirm the order. No one has to check stock levels or remember to reorder.

A spreadsheet and a piece of paper turned into a self-managing inventory system.

Features

Barcode scanner integration, bin-level tracking, item search, quantity logging, reorder threshold alerts, low-stock dashboard notifications, spreadsheet import, AI-powered auto-replenishment emails to suppliers, quote parsing and approval workflow

Built in

3 days (+ AI replenishment layer added after)

Replaces

Spreadsheets, paper logs, Fishbowl, inFlow, Sortly, Zoho Inventory

Saves

Hours of manual counting, eliminates surprise stockouts

CAL

Live

The owner needed a long-term planning calendar — who's assigned to what project, which work is running when, where people need to be next week and next month. Not meetings. Not hourly scheduling. Just a clear, visual layout of who's doing what and when.

Every calendar app he tried was designed for meetings, not work assignments. So he went back to an Excel sheet. But nobody knew when it changed, and updates got lost.

We built CAL in a single day. A clean, visual long-term planning calendar inside the HUB. When anything changes, every employee gets notified automatically. And if they still want Outlook, they can sync it — both calendars stay in step.

Built in a day. Solved a problem the owner had been fighting for years.

Features

Long-term project calendar, drag-and-drop scheduling, automatic change notifications to employees, Outlook calendar import/sync, team-wide visibility

Built in

1 day

Replaces

Excel scheduling sheets, whiteboards, generic calendar apps

Saves

Eliminates confusion over schedule changes; everyone sees the same plan

JOBS

Live

Clients, projects, quotes, expenses, and profitability — usually scattered across a CRM, a quoting tool, a spreadsheet, and an accountant's inbox. JOBS pulls it into one place.

Every project has its own record. Quotes are built inline with an embedded builder and exported as branded PDFs. Expenses get attached directly to the project they belong to. Labour hours (from TIMESHEET) roll up automatically. At any point you can see the profit on a project — not just the top-line revenue.

Replaces three tools with one, and answers the question every owner actually cares about: are we making money on this?

Features

Client records, project tracking, embedded quote builder with PDF preview, expense capture, labour roll-up from TIMESHEET, per-project profit tracking, company contact settings

Replaces

CRM + quoting tool + project tracker + expense tracker

Saves

Hours per week on admin; visibility into project margins
